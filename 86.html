Profiled leads - what, when and how?

What? Househunters register on our site all the time. We actively call them, have a polite chat about their property requirements, find out if they need help with a valuation, get their contact preferences and create them a profile. We then 'push' this profile to your inbox if you have property in the areas where the customer is looking. We call these leads 'Profiled Leads'. We also star rank them and make a big noise if they're proceedable so you can act fast.

When? All of the time! Whilst you're busy selling and letting houses, we're on the phones to the customers trying to get more profiles to you.

How? Once we have created the profile for the customer, we will send it out to your branch email address. Typically the subject will be 'Lead from Radarhomes: Full applicant profile'.

It has been a record month for us for both conventional telephone and email leads, but also for profiled leads. The chances are you've received some in the past week. If you haven't yet, keep a look out!
