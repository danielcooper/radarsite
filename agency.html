Curchods Estate Agents

About us...

Curchods is a Surrey based Estate Agency, founded in 1938. Our two owners, John Slatter and Andrew Dewar, acquired the business in 1990 and set about building on what was already an enviable reputation for professionalism and customer care.

We provide a high level of service from knowledgeable people. Today, our heritage and knowledge of the local markets in which we deal, combined with the benefits of modern technology, delivers a seamless and thorough property service to our clients.
