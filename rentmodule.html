Need Help? Get help.

Our search assistants are real life human beings (based in the UK) who are here to help you find a property. When you speak to our experts, we take the time to get a real understanding for what you are looking for, and will tirelessly work through our extensive database of properties to create a list that matches your requirements.

Social Integration

As well as being able to sign into our site simply using your Facebook, Twitter or LinkedIn account, Radarhomes can (optionally) integrate tightly with these services, to make your home-searching experience more fulfilling.

Account Optional

Although our services works better if you sign in, we recognise that not everyone wants to create an account. If you simply want to browse our properties (and even save properties and custom searches), you still can, no login required.

Draw A Map

Our map technology allows us to display your search results in a unique way. First draw your search map, the see a list of results side by side.

Almost Ad-Free

Because we're owned by our Estate Agent members, we are able to keep our advertising to an absolute minimum. This results in a cleaner and easier-to-use website, with less bias to your property searches.
